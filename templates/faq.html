Frequently Asked Questions

What is HiIsCool?
HiIsCool is a platform that helps high school students connect with local businesses and opportunities.
How can I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking on the "Login" button at the top of the homepage and selecting "Sign Up."
Is HiIsCool free to use?
Yes, HiIsCool is completely free for students. Businesses may have premium options.
